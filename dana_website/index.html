Downtown Austin Together

Home is more than where you live.

It is the people nearby, the streets you walk, the meetings that shape your block, and the small moments that make downtown feel like home.

The Downtown Austin Neighborhood Association (DANA) helps residents understand what is changing, meet one another, and shape the future of downtown Austin.

As a resident-led civic organization, DANA follows city plans, public safety conversations, transportation projects, parks, development, and quality-of-life issues. The work is practical: turn complicated updates into plain language, connect neighbors with the right meetings and decision-makers, and make sure the people who live downtown are part of the conversation before choices are made for them.
