What DANA Does

For the people who call downtown home.

Life downtown changes quickly. DANA helps neighbors know what is happening, meet the people nearby, and speak up when the city needs to hear from residents.

Resident Advocacy

Helping residents follow city decisions and speak clearly about downtown life.

Board information, bylaws, minutes, committees, and member records stay close at hand.

05

Bring People Together

Downtown is easier to love when you know your neighbors. DANA creates simple ways to meet, volunteer, and stay connected.

06

Keep Things Clear

When something important is happening downtown, we put it in plain English and point residents toward the next useful step.
