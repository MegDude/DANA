Home is more than where you live.

It is the people nearby, the streets you walk, the meetings that shape your block, and the small moments that make downtown feel like home.

What DANA Does

Residents deserve a clear way into the conversation.

DANA helps downtown residents understand what is changing, meet one another, and shape the future of downtown Austin before decisions are made for them.

As a resident-led civic organization, DANA follows city plans, public safety, transportation, parks, development, and quality-of-life issues.

The work is practical: translate complex updates, connect residents with the right meetings, and make sure downtown voices are part of the process.
