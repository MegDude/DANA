Who We Are

Neighbors helping downtown feel more like home.

DANA helps people who live downtown understand what is changing, meet one another, and take part when decisions affect daily life.

We Represent Residents

We pay attention to city plans, street changes, public safety, sound, parks, and development so residents are not left out of the conversation.

We Bring People Together

Downtown is easier to love when you know your neighbors. DANA creates simple ways to meet, volunteer, and stay connected.

We Keep Things Clear

When something important is happening downtown, we help translate it into plain language and point residents toward the next useful step.
