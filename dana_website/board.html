Leadership

Meet the neighbors helping downtown work better.

DANA is led by downtown residents who volunteer their time to help neighbors stay informed, connected, and represented in the decisions shaping daily life.

Resident-led by design

Strong neighborhoods do not happen by accident.

The board brings together residents with different backgrounds, skills, and perspectives. Their shared job is simple: listen carefully, keep the organization focused, and make sure downtown residents have a practical way to participate.

How the Board Works

Every decision starts with the same question.

Is this good for the people who live downtown? The board meets throughout the year to discuss neighborhood priorities, upcoming events, public issues, partnerships, and resident needs.

What the board focuses on

  • Listening to residents
  • Tracking neighborhood issues
  • Supporting events and volunteer opportunities
  • Maintaining responsible governance
  • Building relationships with civic partners
