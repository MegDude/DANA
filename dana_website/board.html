Leadership

Meet the neighbors helping DANA run well.

DANA is led by downtown neighbors who give their time and know-how to keep the neighborhood heard.

Board Leadership

Cyrus Tehrani, President

Hanover Brazos

Cyrus works with city staff, partner groups, and fellow board members so downtown neighbors are part of big planning decisions, including the Central City District Plan.

Kimberly Levinson, Vice President

Four Seasons Residences

Kimberly is a connector. She helps bring neighbors, downtown groups, and local partners into the same room, and keeps DANA welcoming for people who are new to the conversation.

Roger L. Cauvin, Treasurer

Plaza Lofts

Roger keeps an eye on the numbers and the long view, helping DANA spend carefully on things that make downtown easier to live in.

Kara Young, Secretary

The Independent

Kara helps make DANA easier to find, follow, and join. Her work focuses on clear notes and a friendly first step for neighbors who want to get involved.

Meg Dude, Director

The Shore

Meg brings a digital strategy lens to the board. She works with fellow directors to make DANA's website, membership path, and everyday tools easier for neighbors to use.

How The Board Works

The board works best when it listens first, keeps records clear, and makes it easier for neighbors to know what is happening.

Right now, that means planning notes, safety, sound concerns, sponsorships, and better ways for neighbors to know where to go next.
