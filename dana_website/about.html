About

DANA helps downtown feel like home.

We help neighbors understand what is changing, meet each other, and speak up for the blocks we share.

Mission

DANA helps downtown residents understand local decisions, speak up on issues that affect daily life, and build a neighborhood where people know each other.

How It Works

We share useful updates, host meetings and gatherings, and help residents bring clear concerns to the people making decisions about downtown.

Governance

Board details, bylaws, minutes, and committee notes are grouped so residents can find what they need without digging.
