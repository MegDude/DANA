Resident leadership for a livable downtown Austin.

DANA gives downtown residents a clearer path to civic information, participation, and advocacy.

Mission

DANA represents downtown Austin residents in shaping policies, places, and decisions that affect daily life in the urban core. This language should be reconciled with the current official mission statement before launch. NEEDS_DANA_REVIEW

How DANA Works

The organization provides resident advocacy, shares neighborhood information, hosts civic events, and creates ways for residents to engage through membership, sponsorship, volunteer work, and committees.
