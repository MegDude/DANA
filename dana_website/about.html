About DANA

We are the neighborhood association for Downtown Austin.

If you live downtown, DANA is here for you. We bring neighbors together, represent residents, and help people stay informed about what is happening around them.

What We Do

Downtown changes all the time. New buildings are proposed. Roads are redesigned. Parks improve. Businesses open. New neighbors move in.

DANA helps residents understand those changes, connect with one another, and have a voice in conversations that shape the future of downtown.

Most importantly, we help turn a busy city center into a neighborhood.

What Matters To Us

Everything we do comes back to everyday life downtown.

  • Housing
  • Public safety
  • Transportation
  • Parks and public spaces
  • Walking and cycling
  • Neighborhood events
  • Community connections

If it affects people living downtown, it matters to us.

How We Work

DANA is run almost entirely by volunteers. Board members, committee members, and residents give their time because they care about the neighborhood.

Some organize events. Some meet with City staff. Some write newsletters. Others simply welcome new neighbors. Every contribution helps.
