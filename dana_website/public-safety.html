Living well together downtown.

Downtown is the heart of Austin's energy. DANA works with neighbors and city leaders to balance a thriving urban core with the high quality of life residents deserve.

Advocating for Harmonious Co-existence

We believe a mix of uses is what makes downtown synergistic, but we advocate for reasonable policies to ensure residents and local businesses can thrive together.

  • Proactive sound standards: DANA advocates for modern sound ordinances with measurable decibel standards such as 75 dBA and 85 dBC, plus tools like sound sentinels and volume limiters.
  • Permitting oversight: DANA participates as an interested party in city permitting, providing evidence-based feedback on renewals so venues are accountable to neighborhood standards.
  • Infill and design: DANA supports multi-aspect residential design and single-stair reforms that allow better window placement and higher-quality urban living.

Safe and Walkable Streets for Everyone

DANA champions a downtown that is safe whether you are walking, rolling, riding, visiting, or coming home.

  • Vision Zero and pedestrian safety: We lobby for protected bike and pedestrian infrastructure, repainted crosswalks, and better lighting at busy intersections like Guadalupe and Cesar Chavez.
  • Smart mobility: The board advocates for reducing traffic lanes on key corridors like East 6th Street to expand sidewalks and create a safer pedestrian environment.
  • Street-level vitality: We oppose waivers that allow above-grade parking to skip pedestrian-oriented screening.

Evidence of Impact

  • I-35 advocacy: DANA is a plaintiff in litigation regarding the I-35 expansion, fighting for additional air quality studies and mitigation measures.
  • Housing abundance: DANA has supported rezoning for specialized senior care and affordable housing projects so downtown remains accessible to people of all ages and abilities.
  • Legislative voice: DANA represents member property rights at the state level and opposes legislation that would override local noise protections.
  • Public safety partnerships: DANA maintains direct lines to APD District Representatives and the city's Sound Enforcement team.

Protecting Your Right to a Restful Night

The most frequent challenge for residents is noise from outdoor bars and music venues. Live music is Austin's soul, and DANA advocates for harmonious co-existence where residents can sleep and businesses can thrive.

  • The challenge: Residents report loud music and vibrating floors well past legal cutoff times.
  • Our advocacy: DANA formally objects to permit renewals for venues that repeatedly break noise laws and pushes for stricter city oversight before and after permits are granted.
  • Smart solutions: DANA recommends sound sentinels and volume limiters to keep levels reasonable without ruining the music.

Safer Streets for Everyone

  • Pedestrian safety: Better lighting, repainted crosswalks, and pedestrian-oriented policies.
  • High-rise security: Best Practices guides for downtown high-rises.
  • Compassionate solutions: Long-term housing and social services as the real solution to chronic homelessness.

Key Insight

A healthy downtown is not just an entertainment district. It is a neighborhood.
